About Us

Parking Pizza is a local pizza shop focused on fresh ingredients, fast service, and great flavor every day.

We also have easy nearby parking, so pickup is quick and convenient whenever you are on the go.

Our dough is prepared daily, our sauces are made in-house, and every pizza is baked for a crisp crust and rich taste.

Whether you are grabbing a quick lunch, ordering for family night, or stopping in late, our team is here to serve quality pizza with friendly service.
